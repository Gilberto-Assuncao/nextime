# NEXTIME --- GO TO MARKET STRATEGY

Version: 1.0 Status: Active Last Updated: 2026-07-21

# Purpose

This document defines the official Go-to-Market (GTM) strategy for the
NEXTIME platform, describing how the product will be introduced,
acquired, and scaled.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Vision

Launch a modern SaaS platform that becomes the preferred workforce and
time management solution for construction and field service companies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Initial Target Market

Primary:

-   Construction companies
-   Electrical contractors
-   HVAC installers
-   Maintenance providers
-   Small and medium businesses (SMBs)

Secondary:

-   Industrial service companies
-   Facility management
-   Cleaning services
-   Logistics teams

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Value Proposition

NEXTIME delivers:

-   Fast and intuitive time tracking
-   Team and project management
-   Secure multi-tenant architecture
-   Real-time operational visibility
-   Scalable SaaS platform

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Customer Acquisition Channels

-   Organic SEO
-   Content marketing
-   Google Ads
-   LinkedIn
-   Industry events
-   Referral program
-   Strategic partnerships

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Sales Strategy

-   Self-service onboarding for SMBs
-   Product demos for larger customers
-   Enterprise sales for strategic accounts

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Onboarding Strategy

Objectives:

-   Account creation in minutes
-   Guided setup wizard
-   Sample data
-   Contextual help
-   Knowledge base

Success Metric:

First business value within the first day.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Retention Strategy

-   Excellent support
-   Continuous product improvements
-   Customer feedback loops
-   Regular feature releases
-   Usage analytics

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Future expansion:

-   Rest of Europe

# Partnerships

Potential partners:

# First 24-Month Goals

-   Validate product-market fit
-   Acquire first paying customers
-   Reach recurring revenue growth
-   Launch mobile applications
-   Expand into additional European markets

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Success Metrics

-   Customer acquisition rate
-   Trial-to-paid conversion
-   Customer retention
-   Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
-   Net Promoter Score (NPS)

# Goal

Provide a structured strategy for launching, growing, and expanding
NEXTIME while aligning product, marketing, sales, and customer success
efforts.
