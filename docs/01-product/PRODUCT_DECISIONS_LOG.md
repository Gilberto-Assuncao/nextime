lifecycle of the NEXTIME platform.

and architectural product decisions.

# Initial Product Decisions

## PD-001

Title: Documentation-first development

Status: Approved

Decision:

Documentation must be completed before implementation of new
functionality.

Reason:

Improves alignment, reduces ambiguity and supports AI-assisted
development.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## PD-002

Title: Small Sprint Delivery

Status: Approved

Decision:

Development will be executed through small, independently deliverable
sprints.

Reason:

Reduces regression risk and improves predictability.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## PD-003

Title: Strict TypeScript

Status: Approved

Decision:

Strict TypeScript mode is mandatory throughout the project.

Reason:

Improve maintainability and reduce runtime errors.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Governance

Every significant product decision should:

-   Receive a unique identifier.
-   Be reviewed before implementation.
-   Remain immutable after approval.
-   Reference any superseding decision.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Goal

Maintain a transparent and traceable history of product decisions,
ensuring consistency as NEXTIME evolves.
