# NEXTIME --- PRODUCT GLOSSARY

Version: 1.0 Status: Active Last Updated: 2026-07-21

# Purpose

This glossary defines the official terminology used throughout the
NEXTIME platform, ensuring consistent communication across product,
engineering, design and documentation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Business Terms

## Tenant

An isolated customer environment containing one or more companies and
their data.

## Company

A business entity managed within a tenant.

## Team

A group of employees organized for operational purposes.

## Employee

A user who records work and participates in projects.

## Manager

A user responsible for supervising teams and approving work.

## Project

A unit of work where employees allocate time.

## Time Entry

A record containing the hours worked by an employee.

## Timesheet

A collection of time entries for a defined period.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Technical Terms

## API

Application Programming Interface.

## SSR

Server-Side Rendering.

## RLS

Row Level Security used to isolate tenant data.

## RBAC

Role-Based Access Control.

## SSO

Single Sign-On.

## CI/CD

Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery.

## SemVer

Semantic Versioning (MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Product Terms

## MVP

Minimum Viable Product.

## KPI

Key Performance Indicator.

## Dashboard

The primary interface showing operational information.

## Workspace

The authenticated area where users perform daily tasks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Naming Conventions

-   Components: PascalCase
-   Variables: camelCase
-   Database tables: snake_case
-   API routes: kebab-case
-   Environment variables: UPPER_SNAKE_CASE

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Abbreviations

-   MAU --- Monthly Active Users
-   DAU --- Daily Active Users
-   MRR --- Monthly Recurring Revenue
-   ARR --- Annual Recurring Revenue
-   CAC --- Customer Acquisition Cost
-   LTV --- Lifetime Value
-   NPS --- Net Promoter Score
-   CSAT --- Customer Satisfaction Score
