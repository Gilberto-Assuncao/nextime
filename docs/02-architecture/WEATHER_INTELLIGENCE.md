# NEXTIME --- WEATHER INTELLIGENCE

Version: 1.0 Status: Planned Last Updated: 2026-07-21

Weather Intelligence is a future decision-support module. It will resolve forecast locations from Site coordinates, retrieve forecasts for planned working days, and generate traceable weather alerts. Forecast snapshots and historical observations should remain distinguishable and retain provider, timestamp, units, and confidence metadata.

The module may estimate weather impact on planned and worked hours, preserve evidence that supports delay explanations, and expose approved summaries to project reports. Later cost and margin analysis may consume those impacts without allowing weather estimates to rewrite authoritative timesheets automatically.

Integration should use a provider-neutral adapter, company-scoped storage, timezone-aware dates, permission-controlled visibility, audit logs, and retention rules. No weather provider or live forecast is integrated in Sprint 3.5.2.
